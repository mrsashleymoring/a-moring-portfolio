About Me

My Journey

Hi, I’m Ashley.

I am a web developer learning everything I can about making beautiful & functional websites. I love to spend time with my wonderful family, visit beautiful places and enjoy life.

I graduated from college with a BA in Psychology from Chaminade University, but never pursued a career in the field. I started working in the restaurant industry while going to school and I never stopped. I’ve worked for some amazing companies that have given me tremendous opportunity to grow as a person and a leader. I have been fortunate enough to work and travel, build a family and meet some amazing people all over the world.

I have always felt that I have been on a lifelong search to find my passion. I never had one thing that I wanted to pursue until I discovered what it is like to create, build, and grow on the web. I started taking classes with Skillcrush and I have enjoyed every minute of it.

My dream job is to work remotely…wherever that may be.
